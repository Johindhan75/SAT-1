Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes.

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 and can typically reach your location within 30-60 minutes depending on your emirate. We prioritize emergency calls and dispatch the nearest available technician immediately upon receiving your request.

We provide comprehensive technical services across all seven emirates of the UAE. Our primary service hub is in Dubai, with response times of 30 minutes. Abu Dhabi and Sharjah have 40-45 minute response times, while other emirates typically receive service within 60 minutes.

Our average response time for quote requests is 2 hours during business hours (Saturday to Thursday, 8 AM - 6 PM). For complex projects requiring site visits, we'll schedule an assessment within 24 hours and provide a detailed quote within 48 hours of the site visit.

Yes, we offer comprehensive annual maintenance contracts (AMC) for all our services including HVAC, electromechanical, plumbing, and facility management. Our AMC packages include preventive maintenance, priority emergency response, discounted rates, and dedicated account management.

All our technicians are fully certified and licensed by relevant UAE authorities. We maintain certifications from Dubai Municipality, Civil Defense, and industry-specific bodies. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay updated with the latest technologies and safety standards.

We accept all major payment methods including cash, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cheques. For corporate clients, we offer flexible payment terms with 30-60 day credit facilities upon approval. All prices are quoted in AED (UAE Dirham).
